🛡️ For Parents

Built For Safety First

We built this app knowing that parents are the gatekeepers. Every feature was designed with your peace of mind as the top priority.

Parental Gate

A math problem only an adult can solve must be passed before any sign-up. COPPA & GDPR-K compliant.

PIN-Protected Portal

A 4-digit PIN locks your parent dashboard. Children cannot access settings, logs, or billing.

Kid-Safe AI Filtering

Every AI response is filtered against violent, political and adult topics before reaching your child.

Screen Time Controls

Set a daily limit: 15, 30, 45 minutes or unlimited. App locks itself when the limit is reached.

Zero Data Selling

We never sell, share or monetize your child's data. Period. Strict privacy, always.

Daily Progress Reports

Get automated daily summaries of what your child learned, earned, and asked about.
